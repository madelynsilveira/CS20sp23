Class Format

Small Group Instruction Supervised Practice Time
Skating Classes consist of a 25 minute lesson and a 25 minute supervised practice. Girls and boys ages 4 – 18 are grouped according to age and ability. Figure, hockey or recreational sharpened skates can be worn but no double runners. Helmets mandatory for ages 4-9 and strongly recommended for all students. Siblings of different age and ability have the opportunity to enroll at the same class time but will have different instructors.
