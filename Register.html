Interested in Instructing?

Approximately 100 Ice Skating Instructors make up the staff of Bay State Skating School. Many have taught at the school for over 30 years as well as being former or present figure skating competitors. Some have played hockey both in school and college as well as coaching or have coached teams. These professionals are dedicated and caring .Their goal is to teach excellent skills, make skating enjoyable and build confidence in their students. Instructors enjoy sharing the love of skating with their students. Instructors belong to different Professional Skating Organizations such as Professional Skating Association (PSA), Ice Skating Institute (ISI), United Figure Skating Association and USA Hockey.
